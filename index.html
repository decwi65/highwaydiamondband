About

Highway Diamond is an entertaining Roots-rock / Americana band playing covers and original material. Featuring female and male lead and back-up voices, the group can deliver a full evening of crowd-pleasing music - Petty, Cash, Blue Rodeo, Lucinda Williams, Grateful Dead, Wilco, Tom Wilson, John Mellencamp among others...

Thank you for visiting our page! Share it with your friends and we'll see you around town...and of course, thank you for supporting live music.
